About Us

The company, Muted, is a fashion company with the focus of providing quality basics for both men and women with a variety of muted colours. Our goal is to provide the opportunity take part and invest in the minimalist lifestyle. Muted is the shop for people to find staple basics for their closet that will last a lifetime. A minimalist would be attracted to Muted because of the type of product it offers. Long-lasting basics in muted colours to support the aesthetic and lifestyle of a minimalst.
