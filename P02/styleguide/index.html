Basic Brand Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

The company, Muted, is a fashion company with the focus of providing quality basics for both men and women with a variety of muted colours. Our goal is to provide the opportunity take part and invest in the minimalist lifestyle.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

Muted is the shop for people to find staple basics for their closet that will last a lifetime.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

A minimalist would be attracted to Muted because of the type of product it offers. Long-lasting basics in muted colours to support the aesthetic and lifestyle of a minimalst.

Persona

Andrew, 24, is a recent graduate of design school and has just moved to LA to start a new life working at a design agency. Since he is starting completely fresh in this new city, he decides to adopt a new lifestyle, the simple lifestyle of a minimalist. His goals are to adopt the minimalist lifestyle, and find clothing that will last long and offered in simple colours. His frusturations include having too many options and cheap material at popular clothing retailers.
