Bertolucci: What does the company do, or offer?

Bertolucci is a high end fashion company that provides a collection of the newest high fashion. They offer both clothing such as pants, shirts, jackets, cardigans as well as accessories such as jewelry, belts, purses, hats, gloves, socks and more. Bertolucci also offers clothing fittings in store, by appointment.


Bertolucci: Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

Bertolucci is a environmentally conscious brand with an eye for the best clothes, intended to last customers years and years as 'investment pieces'. Quality is incredibly important to this brand, as are timeless pieces that customers will be able to wear for years. As a person, the company would be regarded as formal and environmentally friendly.


Bertolucci: Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service

Bertolucci would be enticing to people who are very interested in fashion and want to invest in high quality, timeless fashion pieces. They would also be interesting to those who are environmentally conscious as the brand is invested in only offering pieces that will last years, with free adjustments and repairs to any clothing sold by them for a lifetime


Bertolucci: Quick Personas

James, 26

James is a 26 year old entrepreneur. He believes that dressing well is important to leave a good impression on those that he does business with. He enjoys shopping at Bertolucci as he is able to sure each piece he purchases fits him as it was intended to, due to the custom fitting appointments. Additionally, if the clothing doesn't fit him, he doesn't have to worry about looking elsewhere as the store will custom tailor his chosen pieces to his size.

Lucy, 32

Lucy is a 32 year old bank manager. Lucy enjoys wearing formal clothing to her work, and loves to find fashionable pieces that are work appropriate. However, Lucy is also very environmentally conscious and only wishes to spend money on clothing that she can rely on to look timeless and last long. Lucy loves to shop at Bertolucci because she can rely on high quality materials that will last her a long time. And if she ever has an issue with the clothing, she is happy to trust Bertolucci's lifetime repair policy to have it fixed.


Overall, Bertolucci is mainly targeted to middle and upper class men and women from late twenties until early forties, as this age range is the most likely to enjoy the environmentally conscious mindset of Bertolucci and also be able to afford the high class pieces.


Bertolucci Font Selection:

For Bertolucci, I have chosen two fonts. The first font is the font for the logo. This font is the amalfi coast font from dafont.com

The second font I have chosen is intended for all other text and it is Lato. In order to establish type hierachy with this font, there are thicker versions of the font available, and adjusting size will help with this, as well as create a more cohesive interface.
